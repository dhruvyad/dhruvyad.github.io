BeSafe

Digital Medical Recording and Awareness Service

Designed and coded the Android, iOS, and Web Applications for BeSafe, including the API. BeSafe is a platform that connects students, teachers, school staff and parents by a common need for easy access to medical data, while maintaining a dynamic medical database and an informative Disaster Management Blog that contains useful information and videos on the best practices for surviving a calamity. The medical database is constantly updated with every visit a student makes to the local medical department, and with every visit, the parents/legal guardians of the student are notified through the BeSafe iOS and Android applications with the details of the medical visit. This updation is made very easy through an intuitive user-interface that allows nurses to update the medical database within a few seconds. Once the database is updated, the parents/guardians are automatically notified through an API request.
I received the Innovator of the Year award for coming up with, and developing this project.
